## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

```
FlyConnect is the social network built exclusively for aviation crew — pilots, flight attendants, gate agents, ground crew, and other aviation professionals. Connect with colleagues across airlines, find layover buddies, discover crew-only deals, and build lasting friendships with the people who share your lifestyle.

✈️ FEATURES

CREW FEED
• Share your layover moments, airport shots, and travel stories
• Follow crew from your airline and others
• Discover trending posts from the global aviation community

MEET YOUR CREW
• Swipe-match with nearby pilots, flight attendants, and crew
• Filter by airline, position, and home airport
• Three match modes: buddy (for friendship), travel (for layovers), and dating
• Real-time chat once you match

GROUPS & COMMUNITIES
• Join groups by airline, route, or interest
• Private group chats for your base, your fleet, or your crew
• Share photos, events, and announcements

EVENTS
• Discover crew meetups in your city or on layover
• RSVP to crew parties, training meetups, and industry events
• Businesses can create featured events for aviation audiences

CREW DEALS
• Exclusive discounts at airport lounges, hotels, restaurants, and transport
• Verified business partners offering real savings for crew
• Save and share the best deals with your crew

SAFECHECK
• Share your safety status ("safe", "unsure", "need help") with your network
• Useful for crew on layover in unfamiliar cities
• Voluntary, private, and under your control

DIGITAL PASSPORT
• Track the countries, cities, and airports you've visited
• Beautiful passport view of your travel history
• Show off your routes to other crew

BUSINESS ACCOUNTS
• Restaurants, lounges, hotels, and transport providers can create promotions
• Reach aviation crew directly with geo-targeted offers
• Built-in analytics and event management

🔒 PRIVACY & SAFETY

• Block and report tools on every user, post, and group
• Optional identity verification for authentic crew profiles
• Full control over who can see your location and profile
• Delete your account and data anytime from Settings
• End-to-end encrypted direct messages (coming soon)

🌍 WHO IT'S FOR

FlyConnect is for active aviation crew including:
- Commercial pilots (Captain, First Officer)
- Flight attendants and pursers
- Ground crew and baggage handlers
- Gate agents and customer service staff
- TSA officers and airport security
- Maintenance and engineering crew
- Cargo pilots and charter crew

And for businesses that serve the aviation community.

📍 GLOBAL

FlyConnect is available worldwide. Connect with crew from major carriers: American Airlines, Delta, United, Southwest, JetBlue, Alaska Airlines, Spirit, British Airways, Lufthansa, Emirates, and more.

🆓 FREE TO DOWNLOAD

FlyConnect is free to use. Business accounts may offer paid promotions (always marked as such).

---

Questions, feedback, or support?
→ support@flyconnect.app

By creating an account you agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, available at flyconnect.app/terms and flyconnect.app/privacy.
```

## App Icon Requirements (reference)

- **Adaptive icon:** 512×512 px PNG, 32-bit RGBA
- **Legacy icon:** 512×512 px PNG
- Design should work within a circular mask (safe zone = inner 66%)
- Brand colors: primary #D4F53C (neon yellow-green), dark #1A1D27

- Should visually communicate "aviation crew community"
